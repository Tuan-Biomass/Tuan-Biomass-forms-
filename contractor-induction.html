        High Risk Work

        Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS)

        A Safe Work Method Statement (SWMS) is required for high-risk construction or maintenance activities on this site. Once approved, the SWMS must be reviewed and signed by all workers involved in the task before work begins.

        Tasks on this site that require a SWMS include:

        • !
          Working at heights without collective protection
        • !
          Confined space entry (silos, bins, ducting)
        • !
          Hot work outside the workshop (welding, grinding, cutting, oxy work)
        • !
          Electrical work and isolation activities
        • !
          Major mechanical maintenance (conveyors, presses, elevators, gearboxes)
        • !
          Installation or modification of plant and equipment
        • !
          Structural repairs or fabrication work
        • !
          Maintenance work involving mobile plant for lifting operations (cranes, telehandlers, forklifts)
        • !
          Work around moving machinery or tasks requiring guarding removal
        Before work begins: Submit your SWMS to the site safety contact for approval. All workers on the task must review and sign it on site before starting.

        Documents & Permits

        Your Responsibilities

        • 1
          Provide copies of SWMS or JSAs for all high-risk work before commencing.
        • 2
          Hold and present valid certificates of competency for all plant and equipment you intend to operate.
        • 3
          Ensure your workers are trained in and follow all conditions of permits issued on site.
        • 4
          For multi-day work: conduct daily toolbox talks and communicate any change in conditions immediately to Albioma management.
        • 5
          Non-compliance must be reported to Chris Foster immediately with a corrective actions list before work continues.
